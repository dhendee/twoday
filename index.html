About Twoday.is

Have you ever wanted to keep track of something you do every other day? What about every third day?

Twoday.is is an experiment in non-seven-day “weeks.” Each week has a different period (every two days, three days, etc.) and a sequence of memorable names.

For reference, the sequences are:

Period Names
2 Left, Right
3 Maiden, Mother, Crone
4 North, East, South, West
5 Thumb, Pointer, Middle, Ring, Pinky
6 Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Violet

Examples

Them: “What day is it fourday?”
You: “It’s Eastday!”

Them: “What day is it today?”
You: “Today? Or twoday?”
